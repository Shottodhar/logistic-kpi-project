## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Logistics Analytics solution developed using Python, SQL, PostgreSQL, and Power BI. The objective is to analyze logistics operations, monitor delivery performance, evaluate profitability, and generate actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

The project covers the complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business analysis, KPI development, and dashboard visualization.

---

## Business Problem

Logistics companies need visibility into operational performance, shipping efficiency, delivery delays, and profitability. Without proper reporting, identifying bottlenecks and improving customer satisfaction becomes difficult.

This project helps decision-makers monitor key logistics metrics and make data-driven operational decisions.

## Key Business Insights

* Standard Class shipping generated the highest number of late deliveries.
* Certain regions showed significantly higher delivery risk than others.
* Revenue was concentrated in a few high-performing markets.
* Consumer customers generated the highest revenue and profit.
* Several product categories contributed disproportionately to overall profitability.
* Delivery delays negatively impacted operational efficiency.
